About Us

Our passion for american bbq all started with a tiny smoker and a bunch of hungry mates in the back yard.

After many years working with smoke and fire we’re ready to show you what American BBQ is all about. Match that with exceptional Wanganui grown potatoes and all our own sauces and we can guarantee your taste buds are going to freak out!

We’re based in Wanganui, or at a festival or event (or paddock) near you. But if you’re the jealous type you can have us all to yourselves for your next event, birthday, work-do, or party.
